TV Choice Awards shortlist revealed - and how to vote for your favourite shows

July 29, 2024
blog

THE TV Choice Awards have revealed the shortlist nominations for this year's ceremony in August, with Netflix smash Bridgerton leading the pack.

The popular awards, which celebrate their 26th anniversary this year, honour the nation’s favourite TV shows and stars, voted entirely by an audience of TV fans.

This year’s winners will be announced at the London Hilton on Park Lane on Monday 14 November. Fans will have the chance to watch red carpet footage, plus the reactions of the winning stars as the evening unfolds, via the TV Choice Award’s social media channels.

This year’s TV Choice Awards will be hosted by commedian, Jo Brand.

Hit shows like Bridgerton, Killing Eve , and Squid Game have been nominated for Best Drama, while This Morning is nominated for Best Daytime Show for the seventh year running, alongise The Chase.

In Best Comedy Performance, Ricky Gervais is nominated for his role in After Life, and Siobahn McSweeney in Derry Girls.
