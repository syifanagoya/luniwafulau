How do I delete emails from indeed?

Every job alert email has a link at the bottom of the email that says “unsubscribe.” Click the link to cancel the alert. If you have multiple alerts set up, you must cancel each one individually. Canceling one alert will not cancel all job alert emails you receive.

How do I delete old jobs on Indeed?

If you would like to remove any jobs from your ‘Saved’ folder, you can click the X to the right of that job posting and it will be deleted from your My Jobs page.

How do you edit messages on indeed?

Edit message templates for jobs posted directly on Indeed:

Click Candidates at the top of the page. Navigate to the candidate you wish to contact and select Send Message. Under New Message, click on the template you wish to use/edit. Edit the message accordingly then click on the Save as template under the message box.

As a job seeker, you can close your account by signing into your Account page.

    How do I delete an indeed assessment?

    While you can’t remove assessments from your Profile, you can control whether or not an assessment is visible to potential employers. On your Profile, scroll to the Assessments section and toggle off the visibility of your Assessment result.

    How do you reset Indeed assessments?
