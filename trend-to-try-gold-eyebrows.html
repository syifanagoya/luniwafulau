How to create the perfect gold eyebrow

February 18, 2024
blog

We've had squiggle and braided brows and now golden arches set to be a major trend for the party season. The bold eyebrow is ripe for a makeover and celebrities including Lily Aldridge, Gigi Hadid, and Margot Robbie appear to be at the forefront of a new beauty concept - all have recently stepped out with glimmering eyebrows.

Model Gigi gained plenty of style points when she walked down Giambattista Valli's spring 2017 runway rocking sparkly arches, and the look is finally trickling down to the high street with beauty companies catering with new products. Guerlain has just launched the Gold Light Top Coat Mascara, while Stila has unveiled Magnificent Metals Glitter & Glow Liquid Eye Shadow in Gold Goddess.

And don't think you have to reach for the dye bottle; the trend is easy to achieve by adding a touch of glamorous lustre to your glossy brows after golden eyeshadow has been dusted across them.

Gold eyebrows are the latest beauty trend 

The Midas touch

Gold highlighted brows will instantly update your going out look and you can decide how bold you'd like them to be, either going for a light touch or the full Midas effect.

Unlike some of the other wacky eyebrow trends, the gold brow manages to tread the line between fashion-forward looks and party attire, and you can easily recreate this look at home, with products you probably already have in your cosmetics kit.

Choose your gold

You can combine a gold powder eyeshadow, such as Marc Jacobs' Beauty Style Eye Con No 7 Plush Eye Shadow Palette in Starlet 204 with brow gel like L'Oreal Brow Stylist Plumper to create a futuristic-yet-regal brow line. Just mix the two together and paint onto brows. Or for easy application straight from the bottle, try BBROWBAR's Limited Edition Brow Glitter - Gold Gel. It's fitted with a precision nib that imparts dramatic golden flecks with just one sweep and also holds hair in place without hardening or flaking. It can be worn alone or over your brow make-up. For a luxurious option, Dior's Diorshow Bold Brows winter edition will add a veil of gold to your eye make-up. This product can be used either to create a bold look or to enhance a natural make-up result.
