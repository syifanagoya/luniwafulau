Who is Mark Curry?

What are some notable roles he has played?

Beyond Hangin’ with Mr. Cooper, his filmography includes roles in films like Talkin’ Dirty After Dark, Panther, and Armageddon, and TV shows such as The Drew Carey Show, Fat Actress, and more recently, Family Reunion.
